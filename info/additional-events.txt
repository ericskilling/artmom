## AUArts Class - Autobiographical Comics

**Course:** Autobiographical Comics
**Course Code:** CEDI-703 1
**Instructor:** Rachelle Ymay Skilling
**Schedule:** Thursday, 18:30 - 21:30
**Start Date:** May 7, 2026
**End Date:** June 25, 2026
**Cost:** $404 (incl. GST)
**Program Type:** Personal Interest / Adult Courses

**Description:**
Use your voice and tell your story through comic arts and turn your experiences into meaningful visual narratives. Your instructor will provide guided prompts, individual and group discussions, and constructive feedback to help you shape personal memories, moments, and reflections into short comics. Emphasis will be on authenticity, clarity, and emotional resonance. Build confidence in combining image and text to tell honest, engaging, everyday stories.

**Location:** Alberta University of the Arts (AUArts), Calgary

**Registration:** Sign up through AUArts website

## Modern Love: Mother Care Exhibition

**Exhibition:** Modern Love; Mother Care
**Venue:** The Lebel Mansion, Pincher Creek
**Dates:** Until October 24 (2025)

**Description:**
A series of art about the balance of caregiving. 'Modern Love; Mother Care' explores 'Mother Care', which encompasses both the care directed towards others and the essential care for oneself. It juxtaposes the outward expression of maternal love towards the family with the inward focus on self-care.

**Features:**
- Glow-in-the-dark/phosphorescent artwork
- Neon/blacklight art
- Paper arts
- Explores themes of contemporary motherhood, care for others and self-care

**Related Publication:**
Mothering and Inter/Generational Trauma - available for purchase at demeterpress.org
