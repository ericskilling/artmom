  Rachelle Ymay Skilling is a Calgary-based artist and juried member of the Alberta Society of Artists. Her work explores themes of motherhood, personal narrative, and the everyday moments that define our lives. Inspired by her Dutch heritage, her bold geometric paintings and drawings capture the chaos and joy of modern motherhood.

  She teaches Autobiographical Comics at Alberta University of the Arts (AUArts) and has been featured in New Forum Magazine and Mutha Magazine.
cv_bio: |-
  Rachelle Ymay Skilling is a Calgary-based visual artist whose contemporary, geometric art explores visual narratives about motherhood, caregiving, and domestic life. A graduate of Alberta University of the Arts, she has exhibited at various galleries in Alberta, such as the Art Gallery of Alberta, GM Gallery and The Lebel Gallery. Her graphic memoir narratives and zines have been published by Mutha Magazine, Demeter Press, and Inservice Comics. Rachelle is a juried member of the Alberta Society of Artists and a member of Panel One Comics Society. Rachelle enjoys sharing her passion for the arts by facilitating workshops with Panel One at Sparrow Artspace or at various non-profits such as CARYA and bb4ck, with a focus on graphic memoir narratives.
