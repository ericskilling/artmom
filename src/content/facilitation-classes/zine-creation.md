## Format

- Three 2-hour sessions exploring zine history, layout, and production
- Hands-on collage, design, and folding exercises to create a finished mini-zine
- Discussion of storytelling and visual sequencing for small-format publishing

## Logistics

- Bring scissors, glue, paper, markers, and collage materials
- No previous zine experience required
- Perfect for writers, artists, and makers who love creative storytelling
