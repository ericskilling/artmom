## Format

- Six weekly sessions, 2 hours each
- Live group discussion, drawing exercises, and individual feedback
- Work from life, image memory, and written prompts to turn stories into panels

## Logistics

- Bring a sketchbook, pencils, eraser, and markers
- Suitable for beginners and intermediate artists
- Maximum class size: 12
