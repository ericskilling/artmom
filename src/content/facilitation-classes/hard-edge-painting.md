## Format

- Four 2-hour workshops focused on hard-edge composition and colour theory
- Demonstrations, guided painting exercises, and critique in a supportive group
- Practice using masking and edge control with acrylic or gouache

## Logistics

- Bring acrylic paints, brushes, paper or canvas, and masking tape
- Beginner-friendly with support for all experience levels
- Materials list available after registration
