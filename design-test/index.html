About

Rachelle Ymay Skilling

Artist • Educator • Juried Member ASA

Rachelle Ymay Skilling is a Calgary-based artist and juried member of the Alberta Society of Artists. She graduated from Alberta College of Art and Design (ACAD) in 2003 with a major in Drawing. Her work can be described as bold, geometric personal narratives. The images are inspired by personal experiences or depicting her emotions in relation to a personal situation. Her artwork is a way for her to share her story. The main topics in her art revolve around motherhood and being a woman. Art about motherhood has been historically devalued. Themes of domestic life and the home are not frequently on display on our gallery walls. She challenges this within her art practice and believes there is a continuity between homemaking and art-making. Her paintings document chaos and quiet, the routine and unfamiliar, play and work, intimacy and emptiness, happiness and sorrow.
