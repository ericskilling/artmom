Portfolio

A selection of artworks from 2018 to present.

Light Through Leaves

2025 Watercolor and Colored Pencil

12" x 16"

A return to smaller, more intimate works following the large-scale Reclamation piece. This piece captures the dappled light of a summer afternoon in one of Calgary's many urban parks.

Reclamation

2024 Mixed Media (Acrylic, Found Objects, Natural Materials)

48" x 36"

My largest work to date, exploring themes of environmental reclamation and the resilience of nature in urban spaces. Materials include collected debris from the Bow River banks.

Seasons of the Bow

2023 Acrylic on Canvas

Series of 4 panels, each 24" x 24"

A four-panel series depicting the Bow River through the changing seasons of Calgary, from spring thaw to winter ice.

Memories in Blue

2022 Acrylic and Gold Leaf on Canvas

36" x 36"

An exploration of memory and nostalgia using blues and golds. Winner of the Artpoint Artists' Choice Award.

Community Garden Project

2021 Outdoor Mural Installation

8' x 12'

A collaborative mural project for the Hillhurst Sunnyside Community Garden. This piece incorporated input from local residents about their connection to nature in the city.

Calgary Skyline Study

2020 Acrylic and Ink on Paper

18" x 24"

A study of Calgary's ever-changing sky, capturing the dramatic clouds that roll over the Bow River valley.

Urban Landscapes Series I

2019 Mixed Media on Canvas

30 x 40

First in a series exploring the intersection of urban architecture and natural light. Created during a period of intensive study in color theory.

First Steps

2018 Acrylic on Canvas

24 x 36

My first formal painting after beginning art classes. This piece represents the beginning of my artistic journey and my exploration of color and movement.
