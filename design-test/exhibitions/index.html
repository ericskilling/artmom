Exhibitions

Exhibition history from 2018 to present.

Calgary Collects 2025

Glenbow Museum Calgary, AB

February 2025 - March 2025

Annual exhibition of works from private Calgary collections. Featured Light Through Leaves.

Reclamation Exhibition

Alberta Society of Artists Gallery Edmonton, AB

May 2024 - August 2024

Solo exhibition featuring Reclamation and related works. Extended run in Calgary at cSPACE Gallery.

Artist in Residence Showcase

Calgary Arts Development Calgary, AB

August 2023 - December 2023

Six-month artist residency program. Presented Seasons of the Bow series and conducted workshops.

Blue: A Group Exhibition

Stride Gallery Calgary, AB

September 2022 - November 2022

Group show exploring the color blue in all its forms. Featured Memories in Blue.

Art in the Park

Confederation Park Calgary, AB

July 2021 - August 2021

Outdoor art fair and community exhibition. Participated with live painting demonstrations.

Summer Solstice Exhibition

Harrisberg Gallery Calgary, AB

June 2020 - August 2020

Annual summer exhibition featuring local artists. Featured Calgary Skyline Study works.

Emerging Artists Showcase

Artpoint Gallery Calgary, AB

March 2019 - April 2019

Group exhibition featuring emerging artists from Calgary. First public exhibition showcasing my Urban Landscapes Series.
